What is Linux?
Linux is an open-source operating system developed by Linus Torvalds in 1991.
Why Linux?
Around 90% of servers and applications run on Linux
Free and open-source
Secure and stable
Widely used in cloud, DevOps, cybersecurity, and servers
How Do I Learn Linux?
Practice on cloud platforms like AWS or Utho
Or set up Linux on your local system (VMware / VirtualBox / Dual Boot)
